This is a description of the recipe of grilled cheese

Ingerdients:

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Butter (softened)
  • Cheese (Cheddar, American, or your choice)

Steps:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice, butter side down, in a hot pan.
  3. Add cheese on top of the bread in the pan.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  5. Cook until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Remove from the pan, slice, and serve warm.
