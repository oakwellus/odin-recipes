Avocado Toast

Completed picture of Avocado Toast

This is a description of the recipe of Avocado Toast

Ingerdients:

  • Bread
  • Avocado
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil (optional)
  • Lemon Juice (optional)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  • Egg (optional)

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread until golden.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork.
  3. Spread the avocado on the toast.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and optional toppings.
  5. Enjoy!
