Lasagna

Completed picture of lasagna

This is a description of the recipe of lasagna

Ingerdients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  5. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  6. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese, followed by mozzarella and Parmesan.
  7. Repeat the layers until the ingredients are used up.
  8. Top with extra mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Bake for 40-45 minutes, then let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
